## Our Mission

## The Vision

**ElohimOS** exists to empower those who serve others in the hardest places:

- **Missionaries** working in remote villages without internet
- **Crisis responders** operating in disaster zones
- **Faith communities** serving the poor and marginalized
- **Under-resourced organizations** doing vital work with limited tech budgets

We believe powerful technology should not require cloud connectivity, expensive subscriptions, or compromised privacy.

---

## Core Values

### 1. **Technology Should Serve, Not Dominate**

We reject the surveillance capitalism model where users are the product. ElohimOS runs entirely on your hardware. Your data never leaves your machine. No tracking, no telemetry, no "phone home."

### 2. **Privacy is Sacred**

Every person is made in the image of God and deserves dignity and privacy. We design our systems to honor that by making privacy the default, not an option.

### 3. **Offline-First is Essential**

The most vulnerable communities often lack reliable internet. ElohimOS works fully offline because we build for the field, not the conference room.

### 4. **Excellence Glorifies the Creator**

We build with excellence not for profit, but because our work reflects the One who made order from chaos and light from darkness.

### 5. **Radical Inclusion**

Jesus didn't turn anyone away. Neither do we. Our tools are built to serve everyone—especially those the Church has too often rejected.

## Why ElohimOS Matters

### The Problem

Most AI tools require:
- ❌ Constant internet connectivity
- ❌ Cloud subscriptions and vendor lock-in
- ❌ Surrendering your data to third parties
- ❌ Expensive hardware and ongoing costs

This model excludes the very people who need technology most.

### Our Solution

ElohimOS provides:
- ✅ **100% local processing** — All AI runs on your hardware
- ✅ **Offline-first design** — Full functionality without internet
- ✅ **Privacy by default** — Your data stays on your machine
- ✅ **Mission-ready** — Built for field deployment and crisis response
- ✅ **Cost-effective** — No subscriptions, no cloud fees
